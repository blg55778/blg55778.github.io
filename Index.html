Frankie Muniz's Rise to the Top

I know I am no better than anyone else in this world -Frankie Muniz

In January of 2000, a sitcom came out featuring the actor of our generation, Frankie Muniz. This show was titled Malcom in the Middle, and was based around a family living in the suburbs, and a kid named Malcom who was the middle child. This television show recieved seven Emmy awards, and became a very nostalgic sitcom.

This show aired for about 6 years, and even landed Frankie Muniz an Emmy Nomination. He was in an assortment of television shows and movies, but he turned to other passions in his 20s. He was a member of two bands as the drummer, and also pursed a short career as a racecar diver.

Below is a list of notable television shows and movies that Frankie Muniz has starred in.

Year Movie/ Show
2000 Malcolm in the Middle
2002 Big Fat Liar
2003 Agent Cody Banks
2005 Fairly Odd Parents
2015 Sharknado 3

Although Muniz starred in a plethora of television shows and movies, and even received an emmy nomination, he suffered from many concussions in his youth and unfortunately does not remember a lot about his past. In past interviews Frankie has claimed to not remember filming any of Malcolm in the Middle.
